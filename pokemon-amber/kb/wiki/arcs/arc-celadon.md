# Future Arc: Celadon

**Status:** Seed only. Details TBD.

## Core Threads

### Erika's Gym Leader Path
Erika is working toward officially becoming Celadon's gym leader. The obstacle is unclear but likely involves:
- Internal family pressure (noble family background, expectations around the role)
- External political opposition within Celadon's institutions
- Team Rocket's presence in the city complicating the legitimacy of anyone in power

Amber gets involved in helping Erika solidify her position.

### The Noble Family
Erika comes from an old Celadon family with ties to the city's institutions. Details TBD:
- Is the family itself divided — some members collaborating with Rocket, some not?
- Does Rocket have leverage over the family?
- What does the gym leader title mean in the context of this family's history?

### Team Rocket in Celadon
Rocket has significant presence in Celadon — Game Corner as a front operation, likely deeper institutional reach. Their interest in Erika's position (or the vacuum around it) is part of the arc's tension.

### Vee's Role
Vee is somehow connected to the Erika plotline — possibly:
- Vee has information about Rocket's Celadon operation from its time as a test subject
- Vee as evidence of what Rocket is doing in the city, which Amber uses to support Erika
- Vee's presence during the Rocket escalation (first encounter) is what pulls Amber into the larger Celadon politics

## Two-Visit Structure

**Visit 1 (pre-4Ever):**
- Amber passes through Celadon, encounters Vee
- Gets drawn into the Erika/Rocket situation peripherally
- Helps Vee fight off a Rocket escalation, leaves without recruiting Vee
- Seeds the Erika arc without resolving it

**Visit 2 (post-4Ever):**
- Amber returns deliberately to find Vee
- The Erika arc may be ongoing or reach its climax here
- Amber recruits Vee; the shared Rocket enemy is the foundation

## Open Questions

- What specifically blocks Erika from the gym leader title?
- What is the noble family's relationship to Rocket?
- Does Erika know about Vee's existence / Rocket's experiments in the city?
- Does Amber's help have a cost — does she get pulled deeper into Celadon politics than she wants?
