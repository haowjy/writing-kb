# Arc 10 + 16 --- The Hidden Kingdom (Argaea)

**Arcs:** 10 (Visit 1, Amber age ~16) and 16 (Visit 2, Amber age ~18)
**Status:** Origin and cosmology reworked 2026-06-05 (see below). Arc 10 key beats largely locked. Arc 16 skeleton established; execution details open.

## The Hidden Region

**Name: Argaea.** An entire folded pocket region sealed inside the **Mega Dimension** --- not a village, not an outpost, a whole self-contained civilization. The gateway is an **anomalous region within the Mt. Silver massif**, reached through the dragon gauntlet; Argaea proper exists in the Mega Dimension fold on the other side. Nobody in the outside world knows it exists.

> [!NOTE] **Naming.** "Argaea" derives from the **Argead** dynasty, the royal house of Macedon (Alexander the Great's line) --- a *concealed* play that does not telegraph Prince Alexander's later conquest turn. See `wiki/characters/prince-alexander.md`, `wiki/vocab.md`.
>
> [!FLAG] Full KB sweep pending: `saga-overview`, `index`, `amber-mc-team` still say "the hidden kingdom" / "the kingdom" and should name **Argaea** where natural.

### How Time Works Inside: Stasis *(locked)*

Argaea did **not** live through 3,000 years of continuous history. When it was pulled into the Mega Dimension, it fell into **stasis** --- near-zero subjective time inside. It is *literally the ancient kingdom*, skipped forward to the present rather than a civilization that ruled itself unchanged for three millennia. (This fixes the implausibility of 3,000 years of stagnation, and it makes the origin cohere: the parents' escape attempt and Alexander's present-day emergence are *the same event* seen across a 3,000-year outside gap.) Alexander is **chronologically ancient, biologically a youth.**

### A 1:1 Bond Culture *(locked)*

Argaea predates the modern trainer institution (teams of six, badges, Poke Ball sport) by 3,000 years. It runs on the **ancient idiom: one person, one deep, lifelong bond** --- coexistence, not roster-building (cf. AZ and Floette, same Kalos-war era). This shapes the royal line and the Arc 10 boss fight (see below).

## Origin (~3,000 Years Ago) *(reworked)*

During the Kalos War, **AZ** fires the ultimate weapon --- a life-drain device that devastates the continent.

**The cosmology (grounded in Legends: Z-A canon).** In Z-A, **Darkrai**, amplified by overwhelming Mega Power, forms a hyperspace distortion **out of the subconscious thoughts and memories** of those nearby (canon: "Hyperspace Lumiose"), twisting time, space, and evolution and pushing Pokemon beyond Lv. 100. Argaea's origin is the **same canon engine, transposed**: the accelerant is **AZ's ultimate weapon** instead of present-day Mega Power, 3,000 years earlier. The weapon's energy **overloads Darkrai**, forcing it into its **Mega form** (a trainerless, self-Mega-Evolving Rogue Mega) --- and Mega Darkrai pulls the entire kingdom into the Mega Dimension (a hyperspace woven from dreams) to shield it. The fold locks; stasis sets in; afterward **Mega Darkrai loses all reasoning.** Darkrai has **no royal bond** --- it is the wild force at the heart of the prison.

> [!NOTE] **Terminology.** The realm is the **Mega Dimension** (a **hyperspace** woven from dreams). "Dream/nightmare" describes its *nature*, never used as a place-name. See `wiki/vocab.md`.

**The lunar duo, severed *(locked)*.** The catastrophe splits the **Cresselia/Darkrai lunar duo**: **Darkrai locked *in*** (it made the fold), **Cresselia carried *out*** (with Alexander --- see below). With its counterweight gone, nothing keeps Darkrai's dreamscape from rotting into pure nightmare. This is *why* the realm becomes a nightmare prison and why Darkrai loses its mind.

**The royal bonds *(locked)*.** Per the 1:1 culture:
- **King --- Cresselia** (the dream-warding, healing legendary)
- **Queen --- Shaymin** (the Gratitude/purification legendary; Seed Flare cleanses corrupted land)

Both parents are bonded to *gentle, healing* legendaries --- which makes their imprisonment in a nightmare realm the cruelest possible inversion.

**The escape *(locked)*.** **Cresselia and Shaymin break Alexander out** of the dream and **escape with him.** The **King and Queen stay trapped** --- only Alexander makes it through. The King has now *given away his own partner (Cresselia)* to save his son, and is left to hold the nightmare **alone and partnerless.** That vigil hollows him into the **Mad King** (see Arc 10). The kingdom loses *both* its healers (Cresselia + Shaymin) at once, which is why it rots so completely.

> [!FLAG] **Reseal agent --- open.** After Alexander + the two legendaries get out, the way closes, re-trapping the parents. Undecided whether this is a **self-reseal** (the unstable distortion simply snaps shut --- arbitrary, cruel) or a **deliberate seal** (the parents/King close it behind him to keep the nightmare from spilling into the world --- a second sacrifice). A physical **quarantine** of the Mt. Silver anomaly can layer on top of either.

## Why the Seal Is Failing Now

**Primary crack:** The Mt. Silver Incident --- the legendary-bird rampage (Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres) that ended the Kanto--Johto war, at the kingdom's threshold; Oak stood at that boundary the day the seal cracked. See: `wiki/lore/history/kanto-johto-unification-war.md`.

**Accelerants in Amber's era:** Mewtwo's escape (Ch 1) and the rising tide of legendary activity push the already-failing seal toward collapse during Amber's lifetime.

## Prince Alexander

Escaped prince of Argaea; **chronologically ~3,000 years old, biologically a youth** (stasis). Carried out of the fold by **Cresselia and Shaymin**, his parents' partners --- who become his companions/guardians in exile (an inheritance heavy with his parents' love and their imprisonment). Met early (Arc 1) alongside Alonso. **No mentor/tutor** --- that idea was cut. In Arc 16 he returns with the two legendaries to complete the purification and reclaims the throne.

His later arc (liberation-of-Kanto that curdles into conquest) is the saga's study of *liberation that cannot stop.* See: `wiki/characters/prince-alexander.md`.

> [!FLAG] **1:1 vs two legendaries.** Alexander travels with *two* legendaries (Cresselia + Shaymin) in a 1:1 culture. Frame them as his *parents' entrusted partners/guardians*, not roster-building. Whether he also has a partner of his "own" is open. (Note: two healing legendaries is significant strategic force feeding his endgame conqueror turn --- but they are *purifying/support*-coded, not raw offense.)

## Arc 10 --- Visit 1 (Amber Age ~16)

### Entry: The Dragon Gauntlet

The approach runs through Mt. Silver's dragon-infested passes --- Gible/Gabite/Garchomp dens, Jangmo-o/Hakamo-o/Kommo-o packs. Most travelers turn back without knowing there is anything on the other side. Oak was driven back by a Kommo-o before reaching the gateway at age 17. He never found out what was there.

### The Party *(updated)*

Amber's **own expedition** --- *not* a quest alongside Alexander. Current party:
- **Amber**
- **Kyle Kong** (by Arc 10 ~17, long past the Sabrina arc; "freed from inherited duty, wants something greater" --- the kingdom is his *chosen* adventure)
- **A third member** --- *probably another girl, TBD.* Roster candidates: Beatriz Romero, Kaede, Mary.

> [!FLAG] Third party member unresolved (identity + role/dynamic).

### Mission and Tragic Reveal

Mission: defeat the **Mad King** --- who is **Alexander's father** *(locked)*. He wasn't evil. Bonded to Cresselia, he was a *dream-warding, healing* king; he gave Cresselia away to save his son and has held back the unleashed nightmare **alone** ever since. The stasis-vigil and Darkrai's corruption hollowed him. Beating him removes the last cork. **Mega Darkrai is revealed.**

> [!FLAG] **The King's combat presence --- open.** Cresselia (his 1:1 bond) left with Alexander, so the Mad King has *no partner* at the fight. Working direction: he fights **as a nightmare-corruption channel** --- a man fused to the thing he suppresses --- rather than fielding a team. (This *supersedes* the earlier Aegislash-vs-Tyrantrum "king's ace" discussion, which assumed a separate bond.) Confirm whether he has any other Pokemon or is purely the corrupted vessel.

> [!FLAG] **Defeated-father vs trapped-parents --- reconcile.** The parents are "stuck" (alive, trapped). If beating the Mad King = subduing/freeing rather than killing, Arc 16's purification could reach/free the parents. Resolve how Arc 10's defeat squares with the parents' Arc 16 fate.

**Gardevoir stays.** With Mega Darkrai unleashed and the king's hold broken, **Amber's Gardevoir** chooses to take up the suppression burden --- a **stopgap** holding the line until Cresselia + Shaymin can return in Arc 16. A quiet goodbye. Amber walks back through the dragon gauntlet alone.

### Mega Evolution Unlocked

Amber learns Mega Evolution here, cracking open at the moment of losing Gardevoir. Being inside the Mega Dimension --- the native ground of Mega Evolution --- explains the speed.

> [!FLAG] Mega-unlock reconciliation: the kingdom (Visit 1) is now the primary catalyst. The saga also describes the Sinnoh/Ceruledge confrontation as where Mega "breaks open." Decide: is Sinnoh emotional completion only, or still mechanically relevant?

### Roster Expansion

- **Gible:** acquired from the dragon gauntlet --- the most narratively earned pickup of the saga. Travels to Sinnoh.
- **Ursaluna:** Ursaring evolves inside the kingdom; the time-locked Mega Dimension naturally contains the ancient conditions the Peat Block evolution requires --- resolving the Hisui-artifact problem without external explanation.

### The Oak Payoff

Oak pushed into the Mt. Silver outskirts at 17, was driven back by a Kommo-o before reaching the gateway, and has told a story with no ending for forty years. After Amber discovers the kingdom, she finally tells him what was on the other side.

### Pikachu Handoff

On the road home, Amber finds a Pikachu that won't go in a ball. She recognizes it, brings it to Oak: *"Give it to Ash if he wakes up late."* Ash is 14 --- just starting his journey. See: `wiki/arcs/arc-pikachu-handoff.md`.

### Ends

Amber returns significantly more powerful, carrying Gardevoir's absence and a promise she cannot yet fulfill. Pikachu handed off. Ash's story begins.

## Arc 16 --- Visit 2 (Amber Age ~18)

A chosen return to an unpaid debt. Amber comes back deliberately with a small team of 4--6 people she trusts --- Alonso likely among them. Her first deliberate legendary-class raid.

Alola and the Delta Episode precede this arc; Amber returns with Ultra Wormhole experience, other-world ethics, Aether/Lusamine lessons, and proof that supporting the right specialist can be the right move.

**Goal:** Not exploration. **Purify/reseal Mega Darkrai** via **Cresselia (Lunar Wing) + Shaymin (purification)** --- the two legendaries Alexander carries home --- not a damage race. Free Gardevoir. Fulfill the promise Arc 10 created. **Alexander reclaims the throne.**

**Win condition:** Purification and reseal, not overpowering a legendary-tier opponent. Cresselia + Shaymin are the mechanism.

**Ends:** Mega Darkrai purified and resealed. Gardevoir freed. Alexander reclaims the throne. The last major personal promise resolved before the global war reveals itself.

> [!FLAG] Possible parent-rescue beat (see Arc 10 reconcile flag): does purification free the trapped King and Queen?
