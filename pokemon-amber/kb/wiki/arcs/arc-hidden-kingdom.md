# Arc 10 + 16 --- The Hidden Kingdom

**Arcs:** 10 (Visit 1, Amber age ~16) and 16 (Visit 2, Amber age ~18)
**Status:** Arc 10 key beats largely locked. Arc 16 skeleton established; execution details open.

## The Hidden Region

An entire folded pocket region sealed inside the **Mega Dimension** --- not a village, not an outpost, a whole self-contained civilization. The gateway is **Mt. Silver**; the region proper exists in the Mega Dimension fold on the other side. Time-locked: the ancient world persists inside. Nobody in the outside world knows it exists.

> [!FLAG] **Needs human review** --- Kingdom name: the hidden region is unnamed. Do not invent one.

---

## Origin (~3,000 Years Ago)

During the Kalos War, **AZ** fires the ultimate weapon --- a life-drain device that devastates the continent.

**Mega Darkrai** was not an aggressor. It was protecting the kingdom from AZ's weapon and absorbed the weapon's overload in the process. That absorption forced it into its Mega form --- a Rogue Mega, trainerless and self-Mega-Evolving, far stronger than normal wild Pokemon. While Mega Darkrai still had its reasoning, it gathered with **Cresselia** and the kingdom's first king. Together, the three chose to fold and seal the region into the Mega Dimension --- protecting both the kingdom from the outside world and the outside world from Mega Darkrai.

The seal has held for 3,000 years. Nobody inside knows life beyond its walls. After the sealing, Mega Darkrai **lost all reasoning**.

**Cresselia's role:** She keeps the sleeping Mega Darkrai's dreams from turning to nightmares. This is an ongoing duty: the dreamcatcher function slowly poisons each keeper. The king in Amber's era wasn't evil --- the role hollowed him over the length of his reign.

---

## Why the Seal Is Failing Now

**Primary crack:** The Mt. Silver Incident --- the legendary-bird rampage (Articuno, Zapdos, Moltres) that ended the Kanto--Johto war. The rampage occurred at the kingdom's threshold; Oak stood at that boundary the day the seal cracked. See: `wiki/lore/history/kanto-johto-unification-war.md`.

**Accelerants in Amber's era:** Mewtwo's escape (Ch 1) and the rising tide of legendary activity that follows push the already-failing seal toward collapse during Amber's lifetime. This is why the king finally breaks and Prince Alexander escapes --- effectively the first contact with the outside world in 3,000 years.

## Prince Alexander

An escaped prince of the hidden kingdom. His escape is itself a symptom of the failing seal. Met early (Arc 1) alongside Alonso. In Arc 16, he reclaims the throne after Amber's team completes the purification.

## Arc 10 --- Visit 1 (Amber Age ~16)

### Entry: The Dragon Gauntlet

The gateway to the hidden kingdom runs through Mt. Silver's dragon-infested passes --- Gible/Gabite/Garchomp dens in the mountain passes, Jangmo-o/Hakamo-o/Kommo-o packs in the wilds. Most travelers turn back without knowing there is anything on the other side. Oak was driven back by a Kommo-o before reaching the gateway at age 17. He never found out what was there.

### Mission and Tragic Reveal

Amber and companions fight through the gauntlet and enter the kingdom. Mission: defeat the **Mad King**.

**Tragic reveal:** The Mad King wasn't evil. He was suppressing Mega Darkrai through his bond with Cresselia --- but the duty hollowed him over his reign, and Mega Darkrai's corruption influenced him. Beating him breaks the dam. **Mega Darkrai is revealed.**

**Gardevoir stays.** With Mega Darkrai unleashed and the king's suppression broken, Gardevoir chooses to take up the suppression burden despite the bond. A quiet goodbye. Amber walks back through the dragon gauntlet alone.

### Mega Evolution Unlocked

Amber learns Mega Evolution here. It cracks open at the moment of losing Gardevoir. Being inside the Mega Dimension --- the native environment for Mega Evolution itself --- explains why she learns it so fast.

> [!FLAG] **Needs human review** --- Mega-unlock reconciliation: the kingdom (Visit 1) is now the primary catalyst for Amber learning Mega Evolution. The saga currently also describes the Sinnoh/Ceruledge confrontation as the moment Mega "breaks open." Author must decide: is the Sinnoh beat emotional completion only, or still mechanically relevant?

### Roster Expansion

**Gible:** Acquired from the dragon gauntlet. The most narratively earned pickup of the saga. Travels with Amber to Sinnoh.

**Ursaluna:** Ursaring evolves into Ursaluna inside the kingdom. The time-locked Mega Dimension environment naturally contains the ancient conditions the Peat Block evolution requires --- this resolves the Hisui-artifact problem without needing an external explanation.

### The Oak Payoff

Oak pushed into the Mt. Silver outskirts at 17, was driven back by a Kommo-o before reaching the gateway, and has been telling a story with no ending for forty years. After Amber discovers the kingdom, she finally tells him what was on the other side.

### Pikachu Handoff

On the road home, Amber finds a Pikachu that won't go in a ball. She recognizes it. Brings it to Oak. Says: *"Give it to Ash if he wakes up late."* Ash is 14 --- just starting his journey.

See: `wiki/arcs/arc-pikachu-handoff.md`

### Ends

Amber returns significantly more powerful, carrying Gardevoir's absence and a promise she cannot yet fulfill. Pikachu handed off. Ash's story begins.

## Arc 16 --- Visit 2 (Amber Age ~18)

A chosen return to an unpaid debt. Amber comes back deliberately with a small team of 4--6 people she trusts --- Alonso likely among them. This is her first deliberate legendary-class raid.

Alola and the Delta Episode precede this arc. Amber returns with Ultra Wormhole experience, other-world ethics, Aether/Lusamine lessons, and proof that supporting the right specialist can be the right move. These strengthen the return.

**Goal:** Not exploration. Purify/reseal Mega Darkrai via Cresselia and Lunar Wing --- not a damage race. Free Gardevoir. Fulfill the promise Arc 10 created. Alexander reclaims the throne.

**Win condition:** Purification and reseal, not overpowering a legendary-tier opponent directly. Cresselia and the Lunar Wing are the mechanism.

**Ends:** Mega Darkrai purified and resealed. Gardevoir freed. Alexander reclaims the throne. The last major personal promise resolved before the full global war reveals itself.
