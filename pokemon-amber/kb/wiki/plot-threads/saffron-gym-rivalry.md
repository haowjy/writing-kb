# Saffron Gym Rivalry

The political proxy war between Saffron's two gyms: the ancient Fighting Dojo (currently official pillar status) and the rising Psychic Gym (currently minor, Silph Co.--backed). The Fighting Dojo will lose official status in 2-3 years; the rivalry plays out through their respective heirs --- [[kyle-kong]] and [[sabrina]] --- competing in the U19 Junior League.

## The Two Gyms

### Fighting Dojo (Currently Official)

- **Leader:** Koichi (Kyle's father)
- **Tradition:** Ancient Fighting-type clan, Aura Guardian lineage techniques
- **Current status:** Official Saffron City pillar gym
- **Trajectory:** Losing prestige; younger generation weaker than elders
- **2-3 year timeline:** Will lose official status (predetermined decline)
- See [[places/saffron-fighting-dojo]]

### Psychic Gym (Currently Minor)

- **Leader:** Sabrina's father / family head (TBD identity)
- **Backing:** Silph Co. sponsorship, wealthy family, modern training methods
- **Current status:** Challenger / minor gym (not official)
- **Trajectory:** Rising, modern training advances favor psychic-type research
- **2-3 year timeline:** Will gain official status (replacing Fighting Dojo)
- See [[places/saffron-psychic-gym]]

## Why Replacement is Inevitable

- **Modern training advances** favor psychic-type research
- **Money/resources:** Psychic Gym has Silph Co. backing
- **Results:** Sabrina's generation outperforming Fighting Dojo's
- **City politics:** Wealthy families favor Psychic Gym
- **Science advancement:** Psychic training methods improving rapidly
- **Tradition fading:** Fewer young people interested in Fighting Dojo's ancient methods

## The Proxy War: Kyle vs. Sabrina

### Kyle's Journey

- **Desperate last attempt** to prove Fighting Dojo's continued relevance
- Father (Koichi) sent him out at the standard age (14) to match Sabrina's timeline
- U19 tournament = political proxy war
- **Kyle's loss to Sabrina accelerates or fails to prevent inevitable decline**
- Effort matters for character; outcome already determined

### Sabrina's Position

- 8 badges in Year 1 solo (elite path, no coaching needed)
- Represents the modern future of Saffron training
- Family expects her win to consolidate the Psychic Gym's claim to official status
- Likely outcome: Sabrina wins the tournament

### Kyle vs. Sabrina Tournament Setting

- **Most likely:** Month 12 Sinnoh U19 (year-end, Arc 1 climax)
- Could be earlier if narrative demands (Month 6 Johto U19)
- Both are exceptional 14-year-olds in the tournament
- Both registered before deadline with 8 badges
- See [[trainer-systems/conference-system]] for the rotation system

## Amber's Position

Amber pursues her own 8 badges separately (see [[characters/amber-mc]]). Whether she enters the same tournament depends on:

- Badge count at registration deadline (TBD --- weird incidents disrupt the gym circuit)
- Conference timing
- Her relationship with Kyle (coaching the rival of his proxy war)

She's a tactical asset for Kyle (strategy support that elevates him to 8 badges), not a competitor in the proxy war itself. The dramatic question is whether her support is enough --- and the answer the worldbuilding has decided is *no*. The Fighting Dojo declines regardless.

## Themes This Surfaces

- Some battles can't be won (the Dojo's decline is fixed)
- Tradition vs. modernization
- Effort matters for character even when outcome is determined
- Class/resource advantage (Sabrina's elite backing vs. Kyle's wounded tradition)
