# Post-Kitakami II Dimensional Displacement Bridge

Current planning direction for the fallout after Kitakami II. The emotional shape is fairly strong; the exact cosmology/mechanics are still partly open.

## Locked Direction

- **Placement:** This happens after Kitakami II, not before.
- **Trigger family:** Mega Darkrai, Mega residue, dream residue, and dimensional stress are allowed to produce real tear-in-space consequences.
- **Destination:** Amber is displaced into the **actual mundane real world**, not an alternate Earth where Pokemon exist as reduced pets or hidden fauna.
- **Identity:** She remains physically **Amber**, not "reverted" into her pre-isekai self.
- **World condition:** No Pokemon exist there except as media/games/franchise material.
- **Duration:** Brief. Roughly **around a month**, not a new long-form premise.
- **Narrative function:** This is an emotional wound, identity test, and recontextualization of home. It is not the story changing genres permanently.
- **Extraction:** Aether / Lusamine pull Amber back out through Ultra Wormhole technology, which becomes the bridge into Alola / Ultra Space material.

## What the Real-World Interlude Should Feel Like

The point is not bureaucratic realism. The point is dislocation.

- Avoid a long orphanage / jail / court / paperwork subplot.
- Prefer pragmatic survival beats: possible hospital escape, limited gear, no valid identity, figuring out how to eat/shelter/move for a short period.
- If she has equipment, it should be partly unusable or only marginally useful in mundane Earth conditions.
- Pokemon media should feel **hollow and painful** to her. Games/anime/cards are only a child's map of the living world she knows, not a substitute for it.

## Why This Fits the Existing Lore Direction

Conversation research pointed toward a useful support structure from **Legends Z-A / Mega Dimension / Hyperspace Lumiose** material:

- spatial distortion and dimensional instability around Mega phenomena
- Hoopa / hyperspace language in Kalos-side lore
- Darkrai tied to subconscious thought, memory, nightmare logic, and unstable interior worlds

This supports the idea that **Mega Darkrai plus concentrated Mega/dream residue can produce dimensional fallout** without requiring the story to claim that every mechanic is already fully solved science in-world.

This is support, not a total canon lock on the exact mechanism.

## Open Mechanics

- What exact moment causes the rupture: Darkrai's defeat, seal failure, seal release, resonance with Gardevoir, Cresselia interaction, or something else?
- Does Amber fall straight into the mundane world, or through a brief Ultra-space-like transit first?
- How much of the rupture is caused by Mega Darkrai versus accumulated residue from the seal and the mountain?
- How does Aether detect or target her specifically?
- How quickly does the bridge hand off into Alola material once she is recovered?

## Relationship to Other Arcs

- **Kalos before Kitakami II** remains desirable because it lets Mega Evolution mastery and dimensional lore exist before this rupture instead of arriving as retroactive explanation.
- **Kitakami II** is the story event that earns this fallout emotionally.
- **Alola / Ultra Space** should begin as the recovery and consequences phase, with Lusamine useful but dangerous rather than simply benevolent.
- **Endgame** stays later. This bridge is not the climax; it is part of how the world gets bigger and more dangerous before the climax.
