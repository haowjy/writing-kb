# Silvertail Lineage

*Meowth / Persian* merchant house with both Kanto and Johto branches. Strongest in Kanto markets (Celadon and Saffron); Johto cousins handle textile trade. Critically: this is the family Madame Boss (founder of Team Rocket) was born into.

## Basic Profile

- **Pokemon affinity:** Meowth / Persian
- **Region Base:** Celadon & Saffron markets (Kanto strong); Johto cousins handle textile trade
- **Type:** Merchant house (commerce, not warrior tradition)

## Culture & Rites

- **Coin-Binding:** daily counting ritual
- **Market Trial:** proving business acumen
- Care for their Meowth/Persian partners (treated as valued partners, not just tools)

## Signature Technique

**Silk Step** --- multi-Meowth crowd redirection for courier protection. Used to escort high-value goods through crowded markets without confrontation.

## Notable NPCs

- **Mina:** clinic reformist, genuinely cares for Pokemon
- **Kaito:** courier, pragmatic but decent
- Various merchants throughout Kanto markets

## Politics & Allies

- Transactional ties with [[nightveil]] couriers
- Partnerships with berry clans for trade
- Respected in Kanto commerce
- Wary of [[../team-rocket]] (the family black sheep) using their reputation as cover

## Clan Character

The Silvertails are **normal, decent merchants** --- practical but caring for their Pokemon. The family ranges from warm (Mina's compassionate clinic) to pragmatic (Kaito's efficient courier work). They:

- Treat Meowth/Persian as valued partners, not just tools
- Hold strong business ethics (most members)
- Are deeply embedded in Kanto legitimate commerce

## The Madame Boss Problem

The Silvertail clan has one infamous black sheep: the woman who would become Madame Boss, founder of [[../team-rocket]].

- **Born into Silvertail** but couldn't bond with Pokemon like others
- Emotionally cold, struggled with attachment
- Received Meowth as starter, tried to care for it but was distant/functional
- **Meowth left her** --- found happier trainer who actually played with it, showed affection
- Saw her Meowth happy elsewhere; concluded bonds are fake/transactional (defense mechanism)
- **Founded Team Rocket** using stolen clan knowledge: logistics, breeding, merchant networks
- Clan tried to help her; she rejected them
- Now the clan publicly distances themselves: "She doesn't represent us. We wish she'd stop using our name."

### Why It Matters

- TR's organizational competence comes from stolen Silvertail merchant infrastructure (logistics, networks, breeding programs)
- Madame Boss married into the [[viridian-house]] family for political legitimacy after founding TR
- That marriage produced [[../../characters/giovanni]] --- the dual inheritance (Viridian Gym from father's side + TR from mother's side)
- The clan position is "she doesn't represent us, we wish she'd stop using our name" --- creates ongoing reputational tension
