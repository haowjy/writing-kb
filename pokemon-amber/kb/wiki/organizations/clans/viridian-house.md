# Viridian House

*Ground-types* (Nidoking, Rhydon, etc.). Kanto-exclusive family --- no Johto ties. **Abandoned traditional clan practices generations ago** during Kanto modernization. Now a powerful but ritual-less Ground-type breeding lineage centered in Viridian City. The family controls [[../../places/viridian-gym]] and through Madame Boss's marriage, became entangled with [[../team-rocket]].

## Basic Profile

- **Pokemon affinity:** Ground-types (Nidoking, Rhydon, etc.)
- **Region Base:** Viridian City (Kanto exclusive)
- **Type:** Modern wealthy family, no traditional clan structure

## Culture & Rites

**None.** Family abandoned traditional clan practices generations ago during Kanto modernization. They're now a wealthy Ground-type lineage with industrial breeding programs --- not a ritual clan.

## Signature Technique

Ground-type specialization passed down through family breeding programs (industrial, not ritualistic). The Viridians produce some of Kanto's finest Ground-type bloodlines through systematic breeding rather than spiritual training.

## Notable NPCs

- **[[../../characters/giovanni]]** --- current Gym Leader, Team Rocket Boss
- **Giovanni's father** --- identity unknown ([[../team-rocket]]'s Madame Boss never says publicly; Giovanni suspects a Viridian gym member)

## Politics & Allies

- **Kanto hardliner faction:** Silph Co. executives, Saffron elite, Vermilion industrialists
- **Secretly backs Team Rocket** (the family connection is hidden, but the political alignment is open)
- The family's industrial-modernist philosophy aligns with TR's nationalist movement

## Family Background

The Viridian House has controlled [[../../places/viridian-gym]] for generations --- this provides legitimate political power independent of their TR connection.

### The Madame Boss Marriage

[[../team-rocket]]'s Madame Boss --- a [[silvertail]] black sheep --- married into the Viridian family for political legitimacy and cover after founding Team Rocket. The marriage was strategic on both sides:

- For Madame Boss: Viridian House gave her a respectable family name, separating her public identity from TR
- For the Viridians: the marriage may have been arranged to consolidate power; the Viridian father's identity is deliberately obscured

### Giovanni's Dual Inheritance

[[../../characters/giovanni]] inherited both threads:

- **From father's side:** Viridian Gym position (legitimate political authority)
- **From mother's side:** Team Rocket criminal empire

He was raised with:
- Mother's philosophy: "Bonds are fake, Pokemon are tools" (Madame Boss's defense-mechanism worldview, see [[silvertail]])
- Father's family: functional Pokemon relationships (industrial but not cruel)

The result is a man who can bond with Pokemon pragmatically but not emotionally.

### Organizational Split

- **Madame Boss** runs TR as pure profit enterprise (her Silvertail-stolen logistics, traditional crime)
- **Giovanni** transformed it into nationalist movement (Mt. Silver revenge, Mewtwo project)
- **Tension:** Mother wants money from selling rare Pokemon; Giovanni wants Kanto supremacy and revenge on legendaries
- **Funding split:** Madame Boss's profit ventures + Giovanni's elite nationalist backers

## Story Hooks

- [[../../places/viridian-gym]] = perfect cover (gym leader authority + Team Rocket shadow operations)
- Family legacy of Ground-type specialization (legitimate prestige)
- Connection to nationalist agenda (Kanto hardliners, Silph Co. executives)
- Kanto hardliners fund Giovanni's nationalist TR operations (separate from mother's profit schemes)
