# Saffron Psychic Gym

Saffron City's Psychic-type minor/challenger gym. **Currently not official**, but rising fast --- expected by Saffron elites to gain pillar status within 2-3 years, replacing the [[saffron-fighting-dojo]], unless Kyle changes the public story.

## Leadership & Backing

- **Leader:** Sabrina's father / family head (TBD identity)
- **Family:** Sabrina's psychic family lineage
- **Backing:** Silph Co. sponsorship, modern training methods, wealthy family
- **Notable trainee:** [[../characters/sabrina]] (age 14, generational prodigy)

## Current Status

- Challenger / minor gym (not official Saffron pillar)
- Modern, well-funded operation
- Psychic prodigies (Sabrina's generation exceptional)

## Trajectory

Modern advantages compound:

- Modern training advances favor psychic-type research
- Silph Co. backing provides corporate money
- Sabrina's generation outperforming Fighting Dojo's youth
- City politics: wealthy families favor the Psychic Gym
- Science advancement: psychic training methods improving rapidly

Within 2-3 years: expected to gain official pillar status (displacing Fighting Dojo), but Kyle's eventual victory over Sabrina can halt/reverse that outcome.
