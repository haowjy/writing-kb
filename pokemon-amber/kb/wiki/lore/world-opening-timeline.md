# World Opening Timeline

How the wider world becomes accessible over the saga's timespan. The "gradual world opening" model paces interregional travel by infrastructure and safety capacity, not political declaration.

## Currently Accessible (Year Amber Arrives)

The Big 4 cluster:

- **Kanto, Johto, Hoenn, Sinnoh**
- Established routes between them (though off-route dangerous)
- Regular ferry/flight services
- Safe travel infrastructure (maintained by Rangers + ACE trainers)

See [[trainer-systems/conference-system]] for how these regions coordinate competitive scheduling.

## Not Yet Accessible (Story Start)

- **Unova** --- Western region, geographically distant
- **Kalos** --- different continent?
- **Alola** --- island chain, remote
- **Galar** --- far western region
- **Paldea** --- newest, unexplored

## Why Routes Need Clearing

- Wild Pokemon are dangerous (Mt. Silver incident historical lesson)
- Off-route wilderness is deadly without preparation
- Safe paths require constant maintenance (Rangers patrol)
- New routes = years of exploration + clearing + infrastructure building
- This is why ACE-rank exists: elite trainers who CAN handle unmapped wilderness

## Route Clearing as Career

- **ACE-level work:** Exploration, mapping, clearing
- Pioneering new paths between regions
- Dangerous but prestigious
- Government/League contracts (well-paid)
- This makes becoming ACE meaningful --- not just strong trainer, but explorer/pioneer

See [[organizations/ace-trainers]].

## Gradual Opening Timeline (Rough)

| Era | Status |
|---|---|
| Current (Year Amber arrives) | Big 4 accessible |
| 5-10 years | Unova routes being cleared; Kalos and Paldea becoming more reachable through institutional and exploratory channels |
| 10-15 years | Alola partially integrated: Aether, elite trainers, researchers, shipping, and security networks can be globally connected even if local systems remain uneven |
| 20+ years | Broader normalization of Alola, Galar, and Paldea travel and infrastructure |

This is normal --- world exploration takes generations.

## Uneven Integration Matters

Alola should not be treated as either fully sealed or fully standardized once Amber reaches it later in the saga.

- **Aether**, the **International Police**, League elites, and wormhole researchers can all operate on a global level.
- Ports, shipping, and security networks can be modern enough to matter.
- But island-by-island culture, kahuna authority, informal routes, and Pokemon Center expectations remain less integrated with mainland systems.

This gives later arcs room for both modern institutional reach and local isolation.

## Story Implications

- Arc 1 (Kanto) and Arc 2 (Hoenn): Big 4 only
- Arc 3+: possible involvement in route clearing if Amber reaches ACE level
- New region exploration and uneven institutional integration become possible mid-saga
- Gradual opening pays off as worldbuilding over saga timespan
