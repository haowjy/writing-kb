# Darkrai and Cresselia --- Canon Reference

This page records official Pokemon game and media facts. It is a reference, not saga invention. Saga-specific use is collected at the bottom under [Saga Application](#saga-application).

---

## Mega Darkrai (Pokemon Legends: Z-A)

- A **"Rogue Mega"** --- wild, trainerless, self-Mega-Evolving
- Far stronger than normal wild Pokemon
- Dark type; very high Sp.Atk; immune to Psychic; weak to Fighting, Bug, and Fairy
- Mega Stone: **Darkranite**
- Z-A design echoes AZ's hair --- a kindred-spirit reading connecting two figures warped by something meant to protect *(fan interpretation, not an official statement)*

---

## Darkrai (Core Games)

- Mythical Pokemon; "not intentionally malicious"
- Cannot fully control its nightmares (**Bad Dreams** ability)
- Lunar duo with **Cresselia** (Newmoon Island vs Fullmoon Island)
- New Moon Island sits between dream and reality

---

## Cresselia (Core Games)

- Lunar duo partner to Darkrai
- **Lunar Wing** is the only cure for Darkrai's nightmare infliction
- **B/W2 manor distinction:** the Lunar Wing cures nightmare-infliction but NOT dream-eating or emptiness --- these are separate phenomena

---

## PMD Explorers of Sky --- Darkrai (Non-Canon AU)

*PMD Explorers of Sky is a non-mainline AU. The events below are not part of the official game continuity. Recorded here for thematic and structural reference only.*

- Darkrai is the hidden villain; disguised himself as Cresselia; manipulated via nightmares and amnesia
- Sealed via a dimensional rift
- Later redeemed once his corrupted personality was erased

Thematic and structural precedent this provides:
- Villain-as-Cresselia-disguise
- Sealing via a dimensional rift
- Redemption through purification rather than destruction

---

## Dream Realm

- Gen 5 Entralink/Dream World establish a traversable dream realm within the mainline games
- Fan reading: Z-A's Mega Dimension distortions are adjacent to the dream realm *(not official; not confirmed)*

---

## AZ Connection

- **AZ:** immortal (~3,000+ years); built the Kalos ultimate weapon (life-drain)
- Thematic rhyme: Mega Darkrai's Z-A design echoes AZ's hair; both figures were warped by something originally meant to protect
- AZ stays Kalos-local in official canon; only the thematic rhyme carries into other contexts

## Saga Application

How this canon material is used in Pokemon: Ambertwo:

- **Mega Darkrai as a protective figure turned rogue** by absorbing AZ's ultimate weapon during the Kalos War, rather than an intrinsically malicious entity
- **Cresselia as the ongoing dreamcatcher** maintaining the hidden kingdom's seal; the duty slowly poisons each keeper, including the Mad King
- **PMD's redemption arc** (purification, not destruction) as thematic precedent for Arc 16's win condition --- the goal is purification and reseal via Cresselia / Lunar Wing, not overpowering a legendary-tier opponent directly
- **The B/W2 Lunar Wing distinction** is relevant: Cresselia cures nightmare-infliction; the scale of the kingdom seal involves something beyond standard nightmare suppression
