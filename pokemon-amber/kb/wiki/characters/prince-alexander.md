# Prince Alexander

Escaped prince of the hidden kingdom. Name follows the saga's "A" name theme (Amber / Alonso / Alexander).

## What Is Established

- Met early in the story (Arc 1) alongside Alonso
- His escape from the sealed kingdom is itself a symptom of the failing seal --- effectively the first contact with the outside world in 3,000 years
- Arc 16: reclaims the throne after Amber's team purifies and reseals Mega Darkrai
