## Amber → Hisui / Volo (time travel)

**From:** `hidden-kingdom-arc` brainstorm (2026-06).

**Idea:** On the return trip from the Mega-Dimension kingdom, Amber & co. get
caught up in the **Hisui era** and collide with **Volo** (PLA's secret final
boss). Variant: **Arceus itself brings them** to Hisui.

**Why parked:**
- Adds the **Arceus / Creation-trio mythology** as a *third* cosmic pillar on
  top of the Mewtwo spine and the Darkrai/kingdom spine — scope creep.
- "Arceus brings them" makes Amber a **chosen-by-god** figure, contradicting
  the locked principle *"Amber is NOT a chosen protagonist"*
  (`workspace/current-direction.md`).
- **Timeline mismatch:** Hisui ≈ a few centuries back, not the 3,000-yr
  Kalos-War founding era.

**If revived:** the cleanest hook is the **dimensional/distortion axis**
(Giratina's Distortion World ↔ the folded Mega-Dimension kingdom ↔ dream
realm), using the **accidental-mishap** mechanism (not Arceus-chosen) to
preserve the not-chosen principle.
