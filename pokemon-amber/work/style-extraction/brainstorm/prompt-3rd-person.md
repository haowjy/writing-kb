# Task: Analyze 3rd-Person POV Chapters

Read chapters 6, 13, and 16 in detail. These are written from Fuji's and Oak's perspectives instead of Amber's default 1st person. Your job is to identify how the prose voice changes for these POV shifts.

Also skim the 1st-person chapters so you have a baseline to compare against.

## What to analyze

### Voice differences from Amber chapters
- How does sentence structure differ?
- What's present in 3rd person that's absent in Amber's 1st person (and vice versa)?
- Is the narrator more distant, more literary, more clinical?

### Fuji's POV voice
- What's distinctive about how Fuji perceives and narrates?
- Emotional register — how does grief, guilt, scientific detachment manifest in prose?
- How does his internal world differ from Amber's?

### Oak's POV voice
- What's distinctive about Oak's narration?
- How does authority, concern, political awareness show in prose?
- How does his relationship to Pokemon/the world differ in narration?

### Transition technique
- How do chapters signal the POV shift?
- Is there a different paragraph rhythm?
- Different use of sensory detail?

## Output

Write your analysis to: .meridian/work/style-extraction/brainstorm/third-person-pov.md

Include extensive direct quotes with chapter citations. Compare specific passages across POVs to show the differences concretely.
