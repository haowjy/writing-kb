## Origin myth

- ~3,000 yrs ago: the **Kalos War**. AZ fires the **ultimate weapon** (life-drain).
- **Mega Darkrai isn't evil.** It was **protecting the kingdom from AZ's weapon**, got caught up by it, and **locked itself up** to keep the kingdom safe. <AI>Absorbing the weapon's overload is what forced it into Mega Darkrai — a "Rogue Mega" (trainerless), per Z-A canon.</AI>
- While Mega Darkrai **still had its reasoning**, it + Cresselia + the (first) king **chose to seal/hide the kingdom** — protecting both the kingdom and the outside world. The kingdom has been hidden by this mysterious force, **stable for 3,000 years**; nobody knows life outside. Then Mega Darkrai **lost all reasoning.**
- <rejected>A Mew in the founding pact / as guardian — "we don't need this." The kingdom is NOT tied to the Mew/Mewtwo spine. Amber enters because the seal is failing, not via Mew DNA.</rejected>
