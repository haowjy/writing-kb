This is a canon reference page: official Pokemon game/media facts, NOT saga invention. Label it clearly.

Sections:

**Mega Darkrai (Pokemon Legends: Z-A)**
- A "Rogue Mega" --- wild, trainerless, self-Mega-Evolving
- Far stronger than normal wild Pokemon
- Dark type; very high Sp.Atk; immune to Psychic; weak to Fighting/Bug/Fairy
- Mega Stone: Darkranite
- Z-A design echoes AZ's hair --- a kindred-spirit reading (fan interpretation, not official)

**Darkrai (core games)**
- Mythical Pokemon; "not intentionally malicious"
- Cannot fully control its nightmares (Bad Dreams ability)
- Lunar duo with Cresselia (Newmoon Island vs Fullmoon Island)
- New Moon Island sits between dream and reality

**Cresselia (core games)**
- Lunar duo partner
- Lunar Wing is the only cure for Darkrai's nightmare infliction
- Distinction (B/W2 manor): the Lunar Wing cures nightmare-infliction but NOT dream-eating/emptiness --- these are separate phenomena

**PMD Explorers of Sky --- Darkrai (non-canon AU)**
Label clearly as a non-mainline AU.
- Darkrai is the hidden villain; disguised himself as Cresselia; manipulated via nightmares and amnesia; sealed via a dimensional rift; later redeemed once his corrupted personality was erased
- Provides thematic/structural precedent for: villain-as-Cresselia-disguise, sealing-via-rift, redemption through purification

**Dream Realm**
- Gen 5 Entralink/Dream World establish a traversable dream realm
- Fan reading: Z-A's Mega Dimension distortions are adjacent to the dream realm (not official)

**AZ connection**
- AZ: immortal (~3,000+ yrs); built the Kalos ultimate weapon (life-drain)
- Thematic rhyme: Mega Darkrai's Z-A design echoes AZ's hair; both figures were warped by something meant to protect
- AZ stays Kalos-local in official canon; only the thematic rhyme carries into saga use

**Sources**
- Bulbapedia: Darkrai article; Cresselia article; PMD Explorers of Sky Darkrai
- Game8: Z-A Mega Darkrai
- Pokemon.com: TCG
